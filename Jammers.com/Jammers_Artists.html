Chilled Cow

Chilled Cow is an artist who makes chill lo-fi beats to listen to, perfect for those times of stress and relaxation. Chilled Cow is someone I would recommend.

C418

Well known for producing the soundtrack for Minecraft, C418 makes chill electronic music that sounds amazing. I would recommend it for times of productivity and working.

Imagine Dragons

The Alternative music at its best, making classics like "Radioactive," "Demons," and their great songs like "Thunder." Their music is motivating and fun.

Yeah Yeah Yeahs

My favorite Indie Rock Band that just jams out and makes great post-rock music. Their songs are great and good for jamming breaks.

Cut Copy

I highly recommend checking out the band Cut Copy for their infectious electronic beats and captivating synth-pop melodies that are guaranteed to get you in the right frame of mind.
